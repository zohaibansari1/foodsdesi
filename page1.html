Banaras Silk House

The founder Haji M. Waheedullah Ansari originally from Banaras along with his associates pioneered in this mega city to introducie this 200 year old textile industry upon migration in 1947.

During this transition technology for weaving banarsi fabrics, hand looms, raw materials, artisans and weavers were brought in to settle here in Pakistan.

We are manufacturers and retailers of Banarsi dress material, sarees and suits.
